Donation and Fundraising

Online Donation: You can e-transfer your contribution to Buddhist Society of Ontario by email to torontobd.monastery@gmail.com.

Cheque Payments: Accept donations in the form of cheques made payable to Buddhist Society of Ontario managing the temple activities. Our mailing address is 10 Harrow Street, Mississauga, Ontario, L4T1J3, Canada.

Fundraising Events: Organize fundraising events such as charity dinners, cultural programs, or community gatherings where attendees can donate towards the temple's expenses.

Membership Programs: Establish a membership program where individuals can become regular donors by contributing a set amount on a monthly or yearly basis.

Promote Donation Opportunities: Utilize social media, email newsletters, temple bulletins, and other communication channels to promote donation opportunities and raise awareness about the temple's financial needs.

Tax Receipts: BSO provides tax receipts for donations to encourage charitable giving and compliance with tax regulations.

 BSO maintains transparency in all financial matters by providing regular updates on how donations are being used and ensuring accountability in managing temple funds.

 BSO is implementing these strategies and leveraging various payment methods. So, you can facilitate donations to support the regular activities and expenses of our temple.
